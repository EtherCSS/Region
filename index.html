Quality Assurance Division

Upholding standards, accountability, and continuous improvement in basic education through systematic monitoring and evaluation across DepEd Region VIII.

About the Division

Mandate

The Quality Assurance Division (QAD) is responsible for ensuring that schools and learning centers comply with national education standards, policies, and guidelines.

Scope of Work

QAD conducts regional monitoring, validation, and evaluation of public and private schools to promote quality, transparency, and accountability.
